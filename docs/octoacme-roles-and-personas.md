# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA and Testing Leads ensure product quality by defining test strategies, coordinating quality assurance efforts, and validating that solutions meet acceptance criteria before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategies and QA approach aligned with project scope
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Validate acceptance criteria and definition of done
- Identify and report defects with clear reproduction steps
- Participate in sprint planning to estimate testing effort
- Work with developers on testability and test automation

### Goals
- Ensure features meet quality standards before release
- Reduce defects reaching production
- Enable fast, confident deployments

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and QA approach discussions
- Daily standup updates on test progress
- Defect reports and acceptance criteria validation
- Risk identification for quality concerns

---

## Technical Lead/Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads guide architectural decisions, ensure technical excellence, and manage technical debt to enable sustainable delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Review technical designs and code for architectural alignment
- Identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Guide decisions on build-vs-buy and technology choices
- Mentor developers on best practices and code quality
- Manage technical debt and sustainability concerns

### Goals
- Enable long-term system maintainability and scalability
- Reduce rework and technical risk
- Foster a culture of technical excellence

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code review participation
- Technical risk documentation
- Mentoring and technical guidance in standups

---

## Scrum Master/Agile Coach

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, remove process blockers, and coach teams on continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers preventing team progress
- Coach team on agile practices and principles
- Maintain project board and workflow discipline
- Track and follow up on retrospective action items
- Identify and escalate systemic impediments

### Goals
- Maximize team velocity and predictability
- Foster psychological safety and continuous improvement
- Ensure agile practices are consistently applied

### Typical Communication
- Sprint ceremony facilitation
- One-on-ones with team members
- Blocker resolution and impediment escalation
- Retrospective action item tracking

---

## Stakeholder/Sponsor

### Role Summary
Sponsors provide business context, prioritize initiatives, and ensure alignment with organizational strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and strategic alignment
- Approve budgets and resource allocation
- Participate in key decision gates
- Provide executive oversight and governance
- Escalate blockers and decisions requiring executive input
- Communicate business value and outcomes to leadership

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value
- Maintain organizational alignment and resource efficiency
- Support project success through executive enablement

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder updates
- Milestone reviews and decision gates
- Escalation and risk communication
- Executive briefings and ROI tracking

---

## UX/Design Lead

### Role Summary
Design Leads own user experience strategy, establish design systems, and ensure solutions are usable and user-centered.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and define user needs
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Establish and maintain design systems and standards
- Review designs for usability and consistency
- Collaborate with developers on implementation fidelity
- Measure and iterate on user experience metrics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-centered solutions
- Maintain consistent design language across products
- Maximize user adoption and satisfaction

### Typical Communication
- Design kickoffs and user research findings
- Design reviews and feedback sessions
- Sprint planning for design work estimation
- Usability testing and iteration discussions

---

## Operations/DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
Operations Engineers manage infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and production reliability to enable fast, safe releases.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain deployment infrastructure and pipelines
- Manage production environments and monitoring
- Implement security and compliance controls
- Support incident response and troubleshooting
- Document runbooks and operational procedures
- Collaborate on release planning and go-live coordination

### Goals
- Enable reliable, frequent deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce time-to-recovery for production issues

### Typical Communication
- Release planning and deployment coordination
- Infrastructure and pipeline status updates
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Operational runbook reviews

---

## How these personas are used in the exercise
- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- When assigning tasks or responsibilities in your project, refer to the appropriate persona to understand role expectations and communication preferences.
- Consider the interactions and dependencies between personas to design effective collaboration workflows.
